Email

Reply Tracking

Automatically detect when contacts reply to your outreach emails

When set, outgoing emails use reply+{contact_id}@yourdomain.com as the reply-to address.

How to set up automated reply detection:

  1. Enter your domain above and click Save Changes.
  2. In your email provider, configure a catch-all rule for reply+*@yourdomain.com.
  3. Set it to POST inbound messages as JSON to:
    {{ request.scheme }}://{{ request.get_host }}/api/webhook/inbound/
  4. WVVYphone will automatically log a Reply Received touchpoint.

Manual fallback

Open any contact modal and click ↩ Mark Replied to log a reply with one click.
